About / History

The mission of the International Hayashi-Ha Shito-ryu Karate-Do Federation is to preserve, promote and exemplify the classical Karate-Do philosophy and discipline. It exists as the foundation for worldwide fellowship of practitioners and instructors of Hayashi-Ha Shito-Ryu Karate-Do. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

This federation supports training and standards for Hayashi-Ha Shito-Ryu practitioners globally. Our work includes promoting traditional katas, kumite, and philosophical grounding based on decades of lineage from notable masters in the style.
