International Hayashi-Ha Shito-Ryu Karate-Do Federation

The mission of the International Hayashi-Ha Shito-ryu Karate-Do Federation is to preserve, promote and exemplify the classical Karate-Do philosophy and discipline. About Us

Welcome to the International Hayashi-Ha Shito-Ryu Karate-Do Federation — an organization committed to the principles of classical karate. Our federation fosters growth in discipline, technique, and spirit across global dojos and practitioners.
