International Hayashi-Ha Shito-Ryu Karate-Do Federation

The mission of the International Hayashi-Ha Shito-ryu Karate-Do Federation is to preserve, promote and exemplify the classical Karate-Do philosophy and discipline. It exists as the foundation for worldwide fellowship of practitioners and instructors of Hayashi-Ha Shito-Ryu Karate-Do.
