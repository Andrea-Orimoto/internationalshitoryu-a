Membership

Membership in the International Hayashi-Ha Shito-Ryu Karate-Do Federation is open to qualified instructors, dojos, and practitioners who support the federation’s mission and standards.

Membership provides recognition, access to federation events, and participation in an international community of practitioners.

All members are expected to uphold the principles of Karate-Do and demonstrate respect for tradition and lineage.
