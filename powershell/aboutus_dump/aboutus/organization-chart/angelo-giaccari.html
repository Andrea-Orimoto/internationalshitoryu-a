  • Angelo Giaccari

Angelo Giaccari

  • Born in Rome Italy 1956
  • Degree in Neurophysiology La Sapienza University, Rome, Italy
  • Served in the Italian Air Force
  • 2013 Received 6th. Dan International Hayashi-Ha Shito-Ryu
  • Received Numerous Sports Awards
  • 2011 - 2015 Director over the Lazio Region Area for Shito-Ryu Karate-Do
  • 2015 Vice President of International Hayashi-Ha Shito-Ryu Karate-Do Federation. Responsible to develop IHSKF in the Country of Italy
