Organization

The International Hayashi-Ha Shito-Ryu Karate-Do Federation operates as a non-profit organization dedicated to preserving traditional karate instruction.

The federation is governed by senior instructors and representatives who ensure technical standards and ethical conduct across all affiliated dojos.

Regional organizations operate under the authority of the federation while maintaining adherence to its principles and technical curriculum.
