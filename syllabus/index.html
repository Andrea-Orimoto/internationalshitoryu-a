Syllabus

The International Hayashi-Ha Shito-Ryu Karate-Do Federation syllabus is based on traditional instruction and classical principles. It reflects the technical, philosophical, and physical aspects of Karate-Do.

The syllabus includes kihon, kata, and kumite appropriate to each rank. Instruction emphasizes proper technique, posture, breathing, and spirit.

Students are expected to demonstrate knowledge and understanding of required techniques as outlined for each grade.
