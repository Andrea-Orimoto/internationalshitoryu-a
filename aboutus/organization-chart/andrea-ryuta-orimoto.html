Andrea Ryuta Orimoto

Andrea Orimoto is an Internet professional with 15+ years experience in International Project/Program Management, leading projects, teams and v-teams in a global multi-cultural environment to deliver high profile international online software projects for big Global IT and Media Corporations.

Among his many passions and interests, Andrea enjoys experimenting in the kitchen with the oven, cooking and baking italian specialties for his family and friends. He is also passionate about breath-hold diving and scuba diving, activities that he practices whenever he is close to the ocean.

Andrea currently lives in Kirkland, WA with his wife Yumiko and his little daughter Miki.

  • 1st Kyu Shotokan Karate (FIKTA Italy)
  • Education: Master of Science in Aerospace Engineering (Polytechnic of Milan, Italy)
